Personal Data We Collect

When you answer a quizz, we collect the following personal data:

  • the date and time you started the quizz;
  • the date and time you finished the quizz;
  • the date and time you started and finished each question;
  • the answers you selected for each question;
  • the answer you typed for each question (including the “other” field of some questions);
  • the IP address you were using while starting the quizz;
  • the account you were logged in with, if any.

When you login with your Google Account, we collect the following additional data:

  • your name;
  • the primary email address associated with your Google Account;
  • the picture associated with your Google Account;
  • the date and time of your first login to this website;
  • the date and time of your last login to this website.

See also Google's Privacy Policies.

How We Use Your Data

Personal Data collected from Google is used to identify your account and know who answered each quizz. The picture is only displayed to users allowed to list all users and their quizzes (after: “Supervisors”).

Personal Data collected from quizzes serves four purposes. First, we use them to automatically process and score each quizz you start on the website, and to allow you to see all your old quizzes and progress. Second, we use them to allow Supervisors to view all quizzes made on the platform, including aggregated infos on best and worst understood concepts. Third, we use this data to offer you better quizzes based on your past answers, e.g. by asking questions you badly answered before. This usage is only by request, if you enable the relevant option when you start a quizz. Finally, the IP address is used to prevent abuses and deleted after a year.

Except as provided above, personal data will not be disclosed to third parties. Notably, Google never has access to your results or any other personal information managed by this website. However, they may be disclosed pursuant to a law or regulation or a decision of a competent regulatory or judicial authority.

Any user may request that the data be rectified, completed, updated, or deleted. To exercise these rights, any user may contact the publisher at the contact address provided in the legal notices in this document.

Cookies Policy

We use cookies to operate this website. For full disclosure, we use the following cookies. They are mandatory, as you won't able to use this website correctly if you block them.

csrftoken
Used to secure all forms on the website, preventing anyone to steal your data or impersonate you.
Stays one year maximum.
sessionid
Used to keep you logged in, and for some other usages like flash messages and association of your quizzes to your account when you answer them before sign-in.
Stays two weeks maximum.

We don't use third-party cookies (like analytics).
